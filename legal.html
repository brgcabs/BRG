Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1st June 2025  |  Applies to: www.brgcabs.in

📌 BRG CABS ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information when you use our services:

  • Identity Information: Full name, mobile number, email address.
  • Travel Information: Pickup location, drop location, travel date & time, passenger count.
  • Payment Information: Transaction IDs via Razorpay (we do not store card or bank details).
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, for analytics only.
  • Communication Records: WhatsApp messages, inquiry forms and call records for quality assurance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm, manage and fulfil your cab booking.
  • To send OTP verification for secure booking confirmation.
  • To communicate booking confirmations, driver details and trip updates via WhatsApp/SMS/email.
  • To process payments securely through Razorpay.
  • To improve our services based on aggregated usage patterns.
  • To send promotional offers (only if you have opted in).

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We share data only with:

  • Assigned Driver: Your name and mobile number are shared with your assigned driver.
  • Payment Processor: Razorpay receives billing information for payment processing.
  • SMS/OTP Provider: Fast2SMS receives your mobile number solely to deliver OTP.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or court order.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your booking data is stored securely in Google Sheets (access-restricted).
  • OTPs expire within 5 minutes and are never stored permanently.
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant systems.
  • We use SSL/HTTPS encryption on all web pages.

5. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies to remember your preferences and for analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some features may not work as intended.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of promotional communications at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with relevant data protection authorities.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the details below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Refund Policy

Effective Date: 1st June 2025  |  Applies to all online advance payments

📌 We aim to make the refund process simple and fair. Please read our cancellation and refund policy before making an advance payment.

1. Advance Payment

When you pay a 10% advance online to confirm your booking, this amount is held to guarantee your cab. The remaining balance is paid directly to the driver after completing the trip.

2. Cancellation by Customer

Cancellation Time Refund Amount Processing Time
More than 24 hours before trip 100% Refund 5–7 Business Days
12–24 hours before trip 50% Refund 5–7 Business Days
Less than 12 hours before trip No Refund —
No-show by customer No Refund —

3. Cancellation by BRG CABS

In the unlikely event that BRG CABS cancels your booking due to vehicle unavailability or unforeseen circumstances:

  • You will receive a 100% refund of the advance paid.
  • We will attempt to arrange an alternative vehicle at the same or lower rate.
  • We will notify you at least 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time.

4. How to Cancel

  • Call us: +91 98114 19926
  • WhatsApp: wa.me/919811419926
  • Provide your Booking ID and reason for cancellation.

5. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method (UPI, card, net banking).
  • Razorpay processes refunds within 5–7 business days.
  • Bank processing time may add 2–3 additional days depending on your bank.
  • Refunds for UPI payments are typically received within 1–3 business days.

6. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Advance paid for bookings cancelled within 12 hours of trip.
  • WhatsApp or phone bookings (non-payment bookings) — no money exchanged.
  • Additional charges (tolls, parking, night charges) already incurred.
